    COLOR

    Book | Collage

    In this project, there was one week to make a 32-page, 6 ”x 9” book which could contain as much or as little text as desired.

    I decided to take the “as little text as desired” part of the project as a challenge and made a book about colors where every spread was a collage consisting of as close to one color as possible. This was one of my first forays into maximalism.
